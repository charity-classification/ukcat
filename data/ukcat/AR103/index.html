Languages AR103¶

Languages is a tag in the UK-CAT classification of charities in the UK. It is part of the Arts category.

539 charities have been tagged with this tag. This represents 0.27% of all charities.

Keywords to find charities¶

UK-CAT categories are applied to charities based on a set of keywords, in the form of a regular expression.

The regular expression used to find charities in this tag is:

\b(modern languages?|(welsh|Gaelic|french|german|spanish|polish|arabic|bengali|hindi|russian|farsi|japanese|chinese|urdu|portuguese|the) language|cymreig|gymraeg)\b
