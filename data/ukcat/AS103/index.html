Inner Wheel AS103¶

Inner Wheel is a tag in the UK-CAT classification of charities in the UK. It is part of the Associations category.

269 charities have been tagged with this tag. This represents 0.13% of all charities.

Keywords to find charities¶

UK-CAT categories are applied to charities based on a set of keywords, in the form of a regular expression.

The regular expression used to find charities in this tag is:

\b(inner wheel)\b
