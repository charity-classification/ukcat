Playgroup CC104¶

Playgroup is a tag in the UK-CAT classification of charities in the UK. It is part of the Childcare category.

2,260 charities have been tagged with this tag. This represents 1.12% of all charities.

Keywords to find charities¶

UK-CAT categories are applied to charities based on a set of keywords, in the form of a regular expression.

The regular expression used to find charities in this tag is:

\b(play[- ]?(group|school)s?|mums (and|&) tots)\b
