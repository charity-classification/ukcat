Fundraising CV102¶

Fundraising is a tag in the UK-CAT classification of charities in the UK. It is part of the Charity and VCS support category.

16,076 charities have been tagged with this tag. This represents 7.91% of all charities.

Keywords to find charities¶

UK-CAT categories are applied to charities based on a set of keywords, in the form of a regular expression.

The regular expression used to find charities in this tag is:

\b(fund[ -]?rais(ing|es?)|charitable giving|rais(es?|ing) (funds|money)|attract donations)\b
