Schools ED106¶

Schools is a tag in the UK-CAT classification of charities in the UK. It is part of the Education category.

30,797 charities have been tagged with this tag. This represents 15.29% of all charities.

Keywords to find charities¶

UK-CAT categories are applied to charities based on a set of keywords, in the form of a regular expression.

The regular expression used to find charities in this tag is:

\b(schools?)\b
