University of the Third Age ED109¶

University of the Third Age is a tag in the UK-CAT classification of charities in the UK. It is part of the Education category.

839 charities have been tagged with this tag. This represents 0.42% of all charities.

Keywords to find charities¶

UK-CAT categories are applied to charities based on a set of keywords, in the form of a regular expression.

The regular expression used to find charities in this tag is:

\b(U\.?3\.?A|third age)\b
