Motor Neurone Disease HE211¶

Motor Neurone Disease is a tag in the UK-CAT classification of charities in the UK. It is part of the Health categoryand the Health condition subcategory.

27 charities have been tagged with this tag. This represents 0.01% of all charities.

Keywords to find charities¶

UK-CAT categories are applied to charities based on a set of keywords, in the form of a regular expression.

The regular expression used to find charities in this tag is:

\b(motor neurone disease|mnd|ice bucket challenge|Amyotrophic lateral sclerosis|ALS|Lou Gehrig)\b
