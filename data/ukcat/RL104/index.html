Islam RL104¶

Islam is a tag in the UK-CAT classification of charities in the UK. It is part of the Religion category.

2,559 charities have been tagged with this tag. This represents 1.27% of all charities.

Keywords to find charities¶

UK-CAT categories are applied to charities based on a set of keywords, in the form of a regular expression.

The regular expression used to find charities in this tag is:

\b(islam(ic)?|muslims?|mosques?|quran|MASJID)\b
