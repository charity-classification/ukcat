Spiritualism RL108¶

Spiritualism is a tag in the UK-CAT classification of charities in the UK. It is part of the Religion category.

286 charities have been tagged with this tag. This represents 0.14% of all charities.

Keywords to find charities¶

UK-CAT categories are applied to charities based on a set of keywords, in the form of a regular expression.

The regular expression used to find charities in this tag is:

\b(spiritualis[mt])\b
